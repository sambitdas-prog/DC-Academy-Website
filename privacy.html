Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

At DC Academy, we prioritize the privacy and safety of our students, parents, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy documents the types of information we collect and how we use, store, and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you interact with our website, specifically when you fill out our contact and course inquiry forms. This information may include:

  • Student Details: Full name and target grade level/academic class.
  • Parent/Guardian Details: Parent name (optional) for contact coordination.
  • Contact Information: Phone number (specifically active Indian mobile numbers for direct calls and WhatsApp).
  • Message Details: Any specific requests, previous academic performance, or queries you type into the submission form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect information solely to provide personalized tutoring services. The data is used to:

  • Schedule trial classes and discuss student placement.
  • Send notifications regarding batches, class timings, and exam preparation guides.
  • Address specific student academic requirements in Physics and Mathematics.
  • Directly call or WhatsApp parents to report progress, exam schedules, and results.

3. Data Transfer and Safety

Our website utilizes a secure API bridge (via Google Apps Script connection) that forwards submission details directly to Sir Debabrata Chakraborty's institutional email address.

  • We do not store your personal submission details in a public web database.
  • We do not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your contact details to third-party commercial marketing companies.
  • Access to student files is strictly limited to Sir Debabrata Chakraborty and the internal administration of DC Academy.

4. Cookies and Location Data

Our website displays location directions using an embedded standard Google Maps iframe and displays real review credentials using Google icons. These external integrations may collect IP addresses, device parameters, or load standard analytical tracking cookies in compliance with Google's own privacy framework. We do not run any ad-tracking pixels or marketing beacons on this website.

5. Your Rights and Consents

By using our website and submitting your information on our forms, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to let us contact you regarding academic admission. You may request to review, update, or completely delete your information from our active records at any time by contacting us directly.
